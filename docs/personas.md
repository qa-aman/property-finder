# Personas

**Date:** 24-05-2026
**Status:** Draft, to be validated in discovery

## Primary: The Relocating Outsider

The person moving to a city they do not understand. Knows only the office location.

- **Trigger:** A job in a new city (example: Bangalore to Mumbai).
- **Knows:** Office address, rough budget, transport mode (own car / auto / public).
- **Does not know:** Society names, which areas are good, traffic reality, east/west splits, old versus new societies, the "feel" of an area.
- **Current workaround:** Juggles a listing platform (NoBroker/Housing), an LLM, Google Maps, plus Reddit and Google reviews, and still cannot decide.
- **Frustration:** "These platforms show options but do not empower me to decide."
- **Constraint:** Renting on an 11-month agreement, not buying. Short horizon.
- **Job to be done:** "Give me a few areas and societies I trust enough to go visit."
